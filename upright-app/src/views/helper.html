Upright

ML-powered posture detection

Upright uses your laptop's camera to analyze and help you improve your posture. Now that you have registered and calibrated the system, simply keep Upright open in the background and continue working. Upright will notify you if you begin to slouch so you can correct it. Don't forget to download the Upright iOS app to view your daily metrics. You can now close this window, and Upright will continue to help you improve your posture!
